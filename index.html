Experiences at Sri Lanka

  • Pristine Beaches surrounding Sri Lanka

    Sri Lanka has many prestine beaches around the island such as Unawatuna, Hikkaduwa, Mirissa and more for you to engage yourself in a calming sunbath or an unforgettable surfing experience.

  • Explore the Beauty of Marine Environment

    You can engage with the marine life when you visit to see Dolphins & Whales, and also you get the chance to release baby Turtles to sea while visiting beauitful coral reefs.

  • See the Magical fortress of Sigiriya

    Who wouldn't wish to see the magical Sigiriya Fortress of Sri Lanka, also considered as the 8th Wonder of the World and to be amazed at the anceint technologies used to build the fortress out of a single rock while watching the ancient paintings.

  • Relive the anceint History

    You can visit the ancient sites of Anuradhapura & Polonnaruwa to witness the magestic ruins of the ancient cities that hides many secrets.

  • Train Ride to the famous Nine Arch Bidges

    You can experience a wonderful journey in the train to visit the famous Nine Arch Bridge while visiting Ella - the cold part of Sri Lanka.

  • Go on a hike to the Mountains

    You can relive your dream to hike in the mountains with many places for you to choose from Piduruthalgala, Sri Padaya and many more.

  • Experience the wildlife at rainforests

    Sri Lanka consists of Rain forests nesting many indegenous birds and animals, which is ideal for a bird watching experience.
